## Oric emulators — https://wiki.defence-force.org/doku.php?id=oric:emulators

There exist a number of Emulators for the Oric.

===== Actively Maintained =====

These are still actively maintained by their respective communities.

[[https://github.com/pete-gordon/oricutron|Oricutron]] for Windows, the Amiga, BeOS/Haiku, the Mac, MorphOS/AROS, Linux and the Pandora.

[[https://github.com/tomharte/clk|Clock Signal]] for the Mac.

[[https://www.mamedev.org/|MAME]] for a wide array of platforms (used to be Mess, which was incorporated into MAME).

===== Legacy Emulators =====

These are the old timers. They work, provided you run them on the proper platform, but they are not actively maintained anymore.

[[http://oric.free.fr/emulator.html|Amoric]] for the Amiga.

[[http://oric.free.fr/emulator.html|Arcoric]] for the Acorn Archimedes.

[[http://oric.free.fr/emulator.html|Atoric]] for the Atari ST and descendants.

[[http://iss.sandacite.com/emulator.html|Emul8D]] for Windows.

[[http://oric.free.fr/emulator.html|Euphoric]] for Windows, Linux SVGALib and X, the Mac and Casio Cassiopeias;

- [[http://www.teaser.fr/~amajorel/xeuphoric|X-Euphoric]] the original X port of Euphoric;
- [[http://miniserve.defence-force.org/svn/public/pc/emulators/caloric|Caloric]] a fork of X-Euphoric.
